Tomato Dip

Description

Created from leftovers in the fridge and a bit of boredom, this is a simple dish that combines the acidity of tomatoes with the creaminess of butter. This dish should only take 15 minutes to make for a small batch. Use this recipe as a base to improve upon with your personal taste preference and experimentation.

Ingredients

  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Salt

Instructions

The following instructions are for a small serving.

  1. In a small pot, add 10-20 cherry tomatoes with a couple of table spoons of olive oil.
  2. Stir the ingredients to ensure the tomatoes are covered with a ligh layer of olive oil.
  3. Place the a lid on the pot and place over low heat.
  4. Leave lid closed for 5-10 minutes to trap heat. Shake pot every now and then make sure tomatoes are covered in olive oil.
  5. After the majority of the tomatoes have popped due to the heat, open the lid, and pop the rest of the tomatoes. Let reduce for a bit longer to concentrate the tomatoes.
  6. Add 1/2 tbs of butter and stir. Let reduce to desired consistancy.
  7. Add garlic salt to taste, remove from heat, and serve with bread.

Add your own ingredients to make it to your taste. Examples include:

  • Garlic Clove
  • Basil
  • Cheese
