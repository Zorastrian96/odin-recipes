Protein Shake

Description

It's that time again for to attempt a summer body. The hardest thing isn't lifting heavy but rather makeing sure you get enough protein. Here is a hassle free recipe that will ensure your gains.

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder
  • Almond Milk
  • Banana
  • Ice

Instructions

Video Instructions

The following instructions are for a small serving.

  1. Put in a scoop of Protein Powder.
  2. Add a cup of Almond Milk.
  3. Add some protein.
  4. Throw in a whole Banana.
  5. Add some protein.
  6. For a little taste, add some protein.
  7. Add 1/2 a protein.
  8. Now it wouldn't be a shake without some ice cubes, so we're going to add some protein.
  9. Take some ice cubes and dip them in protein and throw them into the mix.
  10. Add some protein.
  11. Then and only then can you add some protein.
  12. Blend.
  13. Pour the contents into a glass and add some protein.
  14. Now, you can finally add some protein.
    • When you weren't looking, I added some more protein.
  15. Enjoy!

Add your own ingredients to make it to your taste. Examples include:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Chocolate
  • Oatmeal
  • Fruits/Berries
  • Yogurt
  • Protein Powder

If you want to increase the protein concentration, you can do the following:

  • Replace the Almond Milk with Whole Milk.
  • Replace the Banana with Protein Powder.
